<TabItem label='Question'>
:::note[Difficulty: **Easy** - Category: **Structural Analysis**]
A simply supported steel beam spans $L = 25~\text{ft}$ and supports a total service load of $w = 0.25~\text{k/ft}$. The allowable live load deflection is $L/360$. Compute the maximum allowable deflection in inches.
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
Allowable deflection:

$$
\Delta_{allow} = \frac{L}{360} = \frac{25 \times 12}{360} = 0.833~\text{in}
$$

Thus, the maximum deflection permitted under service loads is **0.83 inches**.
:::
</TabItem>
