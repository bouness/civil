<TabItem label='Question'>
:::caution[Difficulty: **Medium** - Category: **Masonry**]
A reinforced masonry wall 8 inches thick is reinforced with #5 vertical bars at 32 inches on center. Determine the steel ratio $\rho = A_s / (b s)$ where $A_s$ is bar area and $s$ is spacing.
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
Area of #5 bar: $A_s = 0.31~\text{in}^2$.

$$
\rho = \frac{A_s}{b s} = \frac{0.31}{8 \times 32} = 0.00121
$$

Thus, $\rho = 0.0012$. This meets typical minimum reinforcement ratios for reinforced masonry ($\rho_{min} \approx 0.001$).
:::
</TabItem>
