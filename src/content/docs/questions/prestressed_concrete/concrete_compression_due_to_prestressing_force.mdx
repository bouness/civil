<TabItem label='Question'>
:::note[Difficulty: **Easy** - Category: **Prestressed Concrete**]
A prestressed concrete beam is pre-tensioned producing an initial tendon force of $P_i = 300~\text{kips}$. The gross concrete cross-sectional area is $A_g = 250~\text{in}^2$. Compute the average compressive stress in the concrete immediately after transfer (ignore eccentricity and other losses). Give the answer in ksi.
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
Average compressive stress:

$$
\sigma_c = \frac{P_i}{A_g} = \frac{300,000~\text{lb}}{250~\text{in}^2} = 1200~\text{psi} = 1.20~\text{ksi}.
$$

**Answer:** $\sigma_c = 1.20\ \text{ksi}$.
:::
