<TabItem label='Question'>
:::danger[Difficulty: **Hard** - Category: **Steel**]
A steel column (A992) with $L = 20~\text{ft}$, $r = 3~\text{in}$, and pinned-pinned end conditions supports $P_u = 120~\text{kips}$. Determine whether **second-order (P–Δ) effects** must be considered per AISC 360.
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
**Compute slenderness ratio:**

$$
\frac{KL}{r} = \frac{1.0(240)}{3} = 80
$$

**Critical stress:**
For $KL/r = 80$, inelastic buckling may occur; check moment magnification:

$$
\frac{P_u}{P_{cr}} > 0.05?
$$


$$
P_{cr} = \frac{\pi^2 E A}{(KL)^2}
$$
 (conceptually)
For typical columns, if $P_u/P_{cr} > 0.05$, **second-order effects must be considered.**
Thus, this column is slender and **moment magnification is required** per AISC 360-16, Eq. C2-2.
:::
</TabItem>
