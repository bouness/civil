<TabItem label='Question'>
:::caution[Difficulty: **Medium** - Category: **Steel**]
Determine the design shear strength $\phi R_n$ of a single 3/4-inch A325-N bolt in single shear with threads included in shear plane. Use $F_{nv} = 54~\text{ksi}$ and $A_b = 0.442~\text{in}^2$, $\phi = 0.75$.
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
Nominal shear strength:

$$
R_n = F_{nv} A_b = 54(0.442) = 23.9~\text{kips}
$$

Design strength:

$$
\phi R_n = 0.75(23.9) = 17.9~\text{kips}
$$

Thus, the bolt has a design shear strength of **17.9 kips**.
:::
</TabItem>
