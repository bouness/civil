<TabItem label='Question'>
:::caution[Difficulty: **Medium** - Category: **Steel**]
A W10×49 column transfers $P_u = 200~\text{kips}$ to a base plate on concrete with $f'_c = 4~\text{ksi}$. Determine the **required plate area** using AISC 360 assuming the plate is uniformly loaded.
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
**AISC bearing limit on concrete:**

$$
R_n = 0.85\sqrt{f'_c}A_1
$$

Factored strength: $\phi R_n = 0.65 R_n$
Set $P_u = \phi R_n$:

$$
A_1 = \frac{P_u}{0.65(0.85\sqrt{f'_c})}
$$


$$
A_1 = \frac{200}{0.5525(63.25)} = \frac{200}{34.9} = 5.73~\text{in}^2
$$

Thus, required plate area = **5.73 in²**, adopt slightly larger plate (e.g., 8×8).
:::
</TabItem>
