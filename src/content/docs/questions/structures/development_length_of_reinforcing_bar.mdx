<TabItem label='Question'>
:::danger[Difficulty: **Hard** - Category: **Structures**]
A **#8 epoxy-coated top bar** $A_s = 0.79~\text{in}^2$ is cast in $4000~\text{psi}$ normal-weight concrete. The bar is spaced 8 inches on center with 3 inches of clear cover. The concrete is un-cracked.
Determine:
The required development length per ACI 318. (Assume all modification factors are 1.0 except for epoxy coating and top bar effect).
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
**Required Development Length**
Using ACI 318 formula for development length:

$$
l_d = \left( \frac{\psi_t \psi_e \psi_s \lambda}{20 \lambda \sqrt{f'_c}} \right) f_y d_b
$$

Given:
$f_y = 60,000~\text{psi}$, $f'_c = 4000~\text{psi}$, $d_b = 1.0~\text{in}$ (#8 bar)
$\psi_t = 1.3$ (top bar factor)
$\psi_e = 1.5$ (epoxy-coated factor)
$\psi_s = 1.0$ (bar size factor for #7 and larger)
$\lambda = 1.0$ (normal weight concrete)
First, compute the product of factors:
$\psi_t \psi_e = 1.3 \times 1.5 = 1.95$

$$
l_d = \left( \frac{1.95 \times 60,000}{20 \times 1.0 \times \sqrt{4000}} \right) \times 1.0~\text{in} = \frac{117,000}{20 \times 63.25}~\text{in} = \frac{117,000}{1265}~\text{in} = 92.5~\text{in}
$$

Thus, $l_d = 92.5~\text{inches}$, or $7.71~\text{ft}$.
:::
