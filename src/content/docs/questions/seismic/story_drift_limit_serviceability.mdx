<TabItem label='Question'>
:::note[Difficulty: **Easy** - Category: **Seismic**]
A building story has clear story height $H = 12.0~\text{ft}$. Code limits story drift to $H/250$ for serviceability. Determine the maximum allowable inter-story drift in **inches**.
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
Convert height to inches: $H = 12.0~\text{ft} = 12\times12 = 144~\text{in}$. 
Allowable drift:

$$
\delta_{max} = \frac{H}{250} = \frac{144}{250} = 0.576~\text{in}
$$

Thus the maximum allowable inter-story drift is **0.576 in**.
:::
</TabItem>
