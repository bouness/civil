<TabItem label='Question'>
:::danger[Difficulty: **Hard** - Category: **Concrete**]
A rectangular beam has $b=10~\text{in}$, $d=18~\text{in}$, $f'_c=4000~\text{psi}$, and two-legged #4 stirrups at $s=6~\text{in}$. Take $f_y=60~\text{ksi}$. Using simplified ACI expressions $V_c = 2\sqrt{f'_c} b d/1000$ (kips) and $V_s = A_v f_y d/(s\times1000)$ where $A_v$ for two legs of #4 is $2\times0.2=0.4~\text{in}^2$, compute $V_n$ in kips.
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
Compute $\sqrt{f'_c} = \sqrt{4000} = 63.2455532$.
Compute $V_c$:

$$
V_c = 2\sqrt{f'_c} b d/1000 = 2(63.2455532)(10)(18)/1000
$$

Compute numerator: $2\times63.2455532 = 126.4911064$. Then $126.4911064\times10\times18 = 126.4911064\times180 = 22,768.399152$.
Divide by 1000: $V_c = 22.768399152~\text{kips} \approx 22.77~\text{kips}$.
Compute $V_s$:

$$
A_v = 0.4~\text{in}^2
$$


$$
V_s = \frac{A_v f_y d}{s\times1000} = \frac{0.4(60,000)(18)}{6\times1000}
$$

Numerator: $0.4\times60,000 = 24,000$. Then $24,000\times18 = 432,000$.
Denominator: $6\times1000 = 6000$.
So $V_s = 432,000/6000 = 72.0~\text{kips}$.
Total nominal shear:

$$
V_n = V_c + V_s = 22.7684 + 72.0 = 94.7684~\text{kips}
$$

Round: **$V_n \approx 94.77~\text{kips}$.**
:::
