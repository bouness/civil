import { Tabs, TabItem } from '@astrojs/starlight/components';

<Tabs>
<TabItem label='Question'>
:::danger[Difficulty: **Hard** - Category: **Concrete**]
A square column $16 \times 16~\text{in}$ supports a flat slab with an effective depth $d = 8~\text{in}$. The factored column load is $P_u = 250~\text{kips}$. The concrete strength is $f'_c = 4000~\text{psi}$. Determine if punching shear is adequate per ACI 318 (use $\phi = 0.75$).
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
**Step 1.** Critical perimeter $b_o$ (at $d/2$ from column face):

$$
b_o = 4(c + d) = 4(16 + 8) = 96~\text{in}
$$


**Step 2.** Shear stress:

$$
v_u = \frac{P_u}{b_o d} = \frac{250,000}{96(8)} = 325~\text{psi}
$$


**Step 3.** Nominal punching shear capacity:

$$
v_c = 4 \sqrt{f'_c} = 4(63.25) = 253~\text{psi}
$$


$$
\phi v_c = 0.75(253) = 190~\text{psi}
$$


**Step 4.** Check:

$$
v_u = 325 > \phi v_c = 190~\text{psi}
$$


**Conclusion:** The slab **fails** punching shear; additional reinforcement or increased thickness is required.
:::
</TabItem>
</Tabs>
