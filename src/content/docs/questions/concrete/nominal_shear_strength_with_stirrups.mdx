<TabItem label='Question'>
:::caution[Difficulty: **Medium** - Category: **Concrete**]
A rectangular beam $b = 12~\text{in}$, $d = 22~\text{in}$ has #3 closed stirrups at 8 in spacing. Each leg area $A_v = 0.11~\text{in}^2$ (2 legs). Concrete $f'_c = 4000~\text{psi}$. Compute total nominal shear strength $V_n = V_c + V_s$.
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
Per ACI 318:

$$
V_c = 2\sqrt{f'_c} b d / 1000 = 2(63.25)(12)(22)/1000 = 33.4~\text{kips}
$$


$$
V_s = \frac{A_v f_y d}{s} = \frac{(0.22)(60,000)(22)}{8} / 1000 = 36.3~\text{kips}
$$


$$
V_n = 33.4 + 36.3 = 69.7~\text{kips}
$$

:::
</TabItem>
