<TabItem label='Question'>
:::caution[Difficulty: **Medium** - Category: **Concrete**]
A short tied concrete column $b = 16~\text{in}$, $h = 16~\text{in}$, with 4% steel reinforcement and $f'_c = 4000~\text{psi}$, $f_y = 60,000~\text{psi}$. Find the nominal axial strength $P_n$ (ignore slenderness).
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
Total steel area:

$$
A_g = 16 \times 16 = 256~\text{in}^2
$$


$$
A_s = 0.04(256) = 10.24~\text{in}^2
$$


$$
P_n = 0.85 f'_c (A_g - A_s) + f_y A_s = 0.85(4000)(245.8) + 60,000(10.24) = 836~\text{kips} + 614~\text{kips} = 1450~\text{kips}
$$

:::
</TabItem>
