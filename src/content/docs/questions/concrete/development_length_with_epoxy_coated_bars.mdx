<TabItem label='Question'>
:::danger[Difficulty: **Hard** - Category: **Concrete**]
Determine the required development length for a #6 epoxy-coated top bar in $f'_c = 5000~\text{psi}$ normal-weight concrete. Assume $f_y = 60,000~\text{psi}$, $d_b = 0.75~\text{in}$, $\psi_t = 1.3$, $\psi_e = 1.5$, and $\psi_s = 1.0$.
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]

$$
l_d = \left(\frac{\psi_t\psi_e\psi_s f_y}{25\lambda\sqrt{f'_c}}\right)d_b
$$

Substitute values:

$$
l_d = \frac{1.3(1.5)(1.0)(60,000)}{25(1.0)\sqrt{5000}}(0.75) = \frac{117,000}{25(70.71)}(0.75) = 49.6~\text{in}
$$

Thus, $l_d \approx 50~\text{inches}$.
:::
</TabItem>
