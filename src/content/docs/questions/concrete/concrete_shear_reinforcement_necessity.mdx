<TabItem label='Question'>
:::danger[Difficulty: **Hard** - Category: **Concrete**]
A beam section has $V_u = 32~\text{kips}$ at the critical section. The concrete shear capacity is $\phi V_c = 45~\text{kips}$. Per ACI 318, minimum shear reinforcement is required when $V_u > \phi V_c/2$. Does the factored shear demand mandate the provision of minimum shear reinforcement?
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
**Minimum Shear Reinforcement Analysis**

**Step 1: Understanding 'mandate'**
'Mandate' means to **require** or **make obligatory**.

The question asks: Is minimum shear reinforcement **required**?

**Step 2: Given information**
$V_u = 32~\text{kips}$

$\phi V_c = 45~\text{kips}$

**Step 3: Check primary condition**
Is $V_u > \phi V_c$?

$32~\text{kips} < 45~\text{kips}$

No stirrups required for strength. But check minimum requirements...

**Step 4: Check minimum reinforcement threshold**
Per ACI 318: Minimum shear reinforcement required when:

$V_u > \frac{\phi V_c}{2}$

Calculate threshold:
$\frac{\phi V_c}{2} = \frac{45}{2} = 22.5~\text{kips}$

**Step 5: Compare demand to threshold**
$V_u = 32~\text{kips} > 22.5~\text{kips}$

The demand **EXCEEDS** the threshold for minimum reinforcement.

**Step 6: Answer the question**
Does $V_u$ mandate (require) minimum shear reinforcement?

**YES** - The shear demand DOES mandate minimum shear reinforcement.

Even though $V_u < \phi V_c$ (concrete alone is adequate for strength), the provision $V_u > \phi V_c/2$ triggers the minimum reinforcement requirement.

**Common trap:** Candidates might see that $V_u < \phi V_c$ and conclude no stirrups are needed, forgetting the ACI minimum reinforcement provision. The word 'mandate' emphasizes this is a code requirement, not optional.

**Step 7: Minimum reinforcement details**
$A_{v,min} = \max\left(0.75\sqrt{f'_c}\frac{b_w s}{f_{yt}}, 50\frac{b_w s}{f_{yt}}\right)$

Or use maximum spacing provisions:
$s_{max} = \min\left(\frac{d}{2}, 24~\text{in}\right)$

**Answer: YES, the shear demand (32 kips) mandates (requires) minimum shear reinforcement because 32 kips > 22.5 kips (which is Ï•Vc/2).**
