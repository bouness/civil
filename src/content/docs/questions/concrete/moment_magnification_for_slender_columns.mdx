<TabItem label='Question'>
:::danger[Difficulty: **Hard** - Category: **Concrete**]
A reinforced concrete column has an effective length $kL = 15~\text{ft}$ and radius of gyration $r = 3~\text{in}$. Determine if moment magnification per ACI 318 slenderness provisions is required. Assume $\delta_{ns} = 1.0$ (no sidesway) and effective height factor $k = 1.0$.
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
Compute slenderness ratio:

$$
\frac{kL}{r} = \frac{15 \times 12}{3} = 60
$$

ACI 318 requires moment magnification for **non-sway columns** when $\frac{kL}{r} > 22$. Since $60 > 22$, magnification **is required**.
:::
</TabItem>
