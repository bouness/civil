<TabItem label='Question'>
:::note[Difficulty: **Easy** - Category: **Concrete**]
A reinforced concrete beam has an immediate (short-term) deflection under sustained load of $\delta_i = 0.25~\text{in}$. Using a simple ACI-type long-term multiplier of 2.0 for sustained loads, estimate long-term deflection $\delta_{LT}$.
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
ACI-style simple approach: long-term deflection = immediate deflection Ã— creep multiplier (here 2.0).  

$$
\delta_{LT}=2.0\times0.25=0.50~\text{in}.
$$
  

**Answer:** $\delta_{LT}=0.50~\text{in}$. 
:::
