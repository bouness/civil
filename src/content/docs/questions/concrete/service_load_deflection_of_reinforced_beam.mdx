<TabItem label='Question'>
:::caution[Difficulty: **Medium** - Category: **Concrete**]
A simply supported concrete beam with span $L = 24~\text{ft}$ has a cracking moment $M_{cr} = 50~\text{kip·ft}$ and service moment $M_s = 90~\text{kip·ft}$. If the immediate deflection under sustained load is $0.5~\text{in}$, estimate long-term deflection using ACI multiplier of 2.0 for sustained load.
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
Long-term deflection = immediate + creep component:

$$
\Delta_{LT} = 0.5 + (2.0 - 1.0)(0.5) = 1.0~\text{in}
$$

Total service deflection is **1.0 inch**.
:::
</TabItem>
