<TabItem label='Question'>
:::note[Difficulty: **Easy** - Category: **Concrete**]
For a 12-inch wide concrete beam with effective depth $d = 20~\text{in}$, determine the minimum area of tension steel $A_{s,min}$ per ACI 318 when $f'_c = 4000~\text{psi}$ and $f_y = 60,000~\text{psi}$.
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
ACI 318 gives:

$$
A_{s,min} = \max\left( \frac{3\sqrt{f'_c}}{f_y} b d, \frac{200 b d}{f_y} \right)
$$

Compute both terms:
1️⃣ $\frac{3\sqrt{4000}}{60000}(12)(20) = 0.252~\text{in}^2$
2️⃣ $\frac{200(12)(20)}{60000} = 0.8~\text{in}^2$
Take maximum → $A_{s,min} = 0.8~\text{in}^2$.
:::
</TabItem>
