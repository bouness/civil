<TabItem label='Question'>
:::note[Difficulty: **Easy** - Category: **Concrete**]
ACI commonly limits the maximum spacing of temperature-and-shrinkage reinforcement to $3h$ where $h$ is slab thickness. For a slab thickness $h = 6~\text{in}$, compute the maximum spacing in inches and feet.
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
Maximum spacing = $3h = 3\times6 = 18~\text{in}$.  
Convert to feet: $18~\text{in} = 1.5~\text{ft}$.  

**Answer:** Maximum spacing = **18 in (1.5 ft)**.
:::
</TabItem>
