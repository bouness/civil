:::note[Difficulty: **Easy** - Category: **Concrete**]
Compute the ACI minimum flexural reinforcement ratio $\rho_{min}$ for nonprestressed concrete with $f'_c = 4000~\text{psi}$ and $f_y = 60,000~\text{psi}$. Use the ACI expression 
$$
\rho_{min}=\max\left(\dfrac{3\sqrt{f'_c}}{f_y},\dfrac{200}{f_y}\right).
$$
 Give the ratio as a decimal and as percent.
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
Compute each term:  
First term: $3\sqrt{f'_c}/f_y = 3(63.2456)/60,000 = 189.7368/60,000 = 0.0031623.$  
Second term: $200/60,000 = 0.0033333.$  
Take the larger: $\rho_{min}=0.0033333$.  
As percent: $0.0033333\times100=0.33333\%\approx0.333\%$.  

**Answer:** $\rho_{min}=0.00333$ (â‰ˆ **0.333%**).
:::
