<TabItem label='Question'>
:::note[Difficulty: **Easy** - Category: **Concrete**]
According to **ACI 318**, what is the **minimum flexural reinforcement ratio** $\rho_{min}$ for a non-prestressed concrete slab with Grade 60 steel and $f'_c = 4000~\text{psi}$?
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
Per ACI 318:

$$
\rho_{min} = \max\left( \frac{3\sqrt{f'_c}}{f_y}, \frac{200}{f_y} \right)
$$

Substitute values:

$$
\frac{3\sqrt{4000}}{60,000} = 0.00316
$$


$$
\frac{200}{60,000} = 0.00333
$$

Take maximum: $\rho_{min} = 0.00333$
Thus, **minimum reinforcement ratio = 0.0033 (0.33%)**.
:::
</TabItem>
