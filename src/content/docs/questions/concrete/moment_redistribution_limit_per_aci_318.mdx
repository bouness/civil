<TabItem label='Question'>
:::tip[Difficulty: **Conceptual** - Category: **Concrete**]
According to **ACI 318**, the amount of **moment redistribution** permitted in a continuous concrete member is limited to what percentage?
:::
</TabItem>

<TabItem label='Solution'>
:::tip[Solution]
ACI 318 Section 6.6.5 allows **moment redistribution up to 20%**, provided that the section satisfies ductility requirements ($\varepsilon_t \ge 0.0075$).
:::
</TabItem>
